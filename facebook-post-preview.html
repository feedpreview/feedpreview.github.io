FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Facebook post preview tool.

Is the Facebook post preview tool free? +
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. Use it as many times as you want with no limitations.
Does it upload my images or text anywhere? +
Never. All previewing happens entirely inside your browser. Your images, text, and data are never uploaded to any server. Total privacy guaranteed.
How many photos can I add to the preview? +
You can upload up to 10 photos. They are arranged automatically in Facebook's native grid layouts (1, 2, 3, 4, or 5+ photo configurations), or you can manually select the grid style.
Can I preview Facebook dark mode? +
Yes! Switch to Dark Mode under the Style tab to see exactly how your post looks for users who have Facebook's dark theme enabled on their device.
Can I download the post preview as an image? +
Yes. Click the Screenshot button at the top of the preview area to download a PNG of your post. The tool uses html2canvas to capture the rendered preview.
How accurate is the preview compared to real Facebook? +
Our preview is built to closely mirror Facebook's actual post design including typography, spacing, reaction icons, comment layout, and photo grids. Minor visual differences may exist due to platform updates.
Can I preview RTL (Arabic, Hebrew) posts? +
Yes. Go to the Style tab and switch Language Direction to "Right to Left (RTL)" to preview how your post reads in RTL languages like Arabic or Hebrew.
