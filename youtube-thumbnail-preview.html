FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the YouTube thumbnail preview tool.

Is this YouTube thumbnail preview tool free? +
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees, no watermarks.
What is the best YouTube thumbnail size? +
YouTube recommends 1280×720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio). The maximum file size is 2MB. JPG is the most common format used by creators.
Can I compare two thumbnails side by side? +
Yes. Upload your first thumbnail in the Thumb tab, then upload a second thumbnail in the A/B tab. Click the A/B context button to see both side by side in a realistic YouTube card layout.
Can I see my thumbnail in YouTube dark mode? +
Yes. Click the 🌙 Dark button in the Style tab to switch all preview contexts to YouTube's dark theme.
Can I add text directly on my thumbnail? +
Yes. Go to the Overlay tab and enter your text. You have full control over font size, weight, color, drop shadow, outline, background style, and 9-point position.
Does the tool upload my image to a server? +
Never. All processing happens entirely inside your browser. Your thumbnail images never leave your device.
Can I preview YouTube Shorts thumbnails? +
Yes. Click the ⚡ Shorts context to see a vertical grid preview of how your thumbnail appears in the YouTube Shorts shelf.
What is the safe-zone grid overlay? +
The safe-zone grid shows the inner 86% of your thumbnail that is always visible — the outer edges can be cropped on certain devices. Enable it in the Thumb tab to ensure your key visual elements are inside the safe area.
