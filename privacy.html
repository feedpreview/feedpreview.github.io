Privacy Policy

Last modified: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FeedPreview. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

2. No Data Storage

Unlike many other social media tools, FeedPreview is designed to be a client-side application. This means that the images, text, and drafts you upload to our preview tools are processed locally in your browser. We do not store, host, or save your content on our servers.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to improve our service, primarily through standard log files and analytics. This may include IP addresses, browser types, and referring pages. This data is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. These third-party services may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your activities across different websites.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
