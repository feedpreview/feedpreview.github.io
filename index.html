FAQ

Common Questions.

Everything you need to know about FeedPreview.

What is FeedPreview? +
FeedPreview is a free browser-based tool that lets you see exactly how your social media posts will appear on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok — before you publish them.
Is FeedPreview really free? +
Yes, completely. No subscription, no premium tier, no hidden fees. All preview tools on FeedPreview are 100% free to use with no signup required.
Which platforms are supported? +
Currently FeedPreview supports Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube. We're actively adding more platform previews based on user requests.
Are my images and captions stored? +
No. All previews are generated entirely within your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, and your captions are never transmitted or stored anywhere.
How accurate are the previews? +
We strive for high accuracy, faithfully replicating platform fonts, layouts, character limits, and truncation rules. Previews are updated regularly as platforms evolve their interfaces.
Do I need to create an account? +
Not at all! FeedPreview is completely account-free. Just open the tool, enter your content, and your preview is ready instantly.
