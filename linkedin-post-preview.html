FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the LinkedIn post preview tool.

Is the LinkedIn post preview tool free? +
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. Use it as many times as you want with no limitations.
Does it upload my images or text anywhere? +
Never. All previewing happens entirely inside your browser. Your images, text, and data are never uploaded to any server. Total privacy guaranteed.
How many photos can I add to the preview? +
You can upload up to 10 photos. They are arranged automatically in LinkedIn's native grid layouts (1, 2, 3, 4, or 5+ photo configurations), or you can manually select the layout.
Can I preview LinkedIn dark mode? +
Yes! Switch to Dark Mode under the Style tab to see exactly how your post looks for users who have LinkedIn's dark theme enabled.
Can I preview LinkedIn polls, articles, and documents? +
Yes. In the Style tab, change the Content Type to Article, Document/PDF, Poll, or Event. Each content type reveals its own customization fields in the Content tab.
Can I download the post preview as an image? +
Yes. Click the Screenshot button to download a high-quality PNG of your post preview. The tool uses html2canvas to capture the rendered preview.
How accurate is the preview compared to real LinkedIn? +
Our preview closely mirrors LinkedIn's actual post design including typography, spacing, reaction icons, comment layout, and photo grids. Minor differences may exist due to LinkedIn platform updates.
Can I preview RTL (Arabic, Hebrew) posts? +
Yes. Go to the Style tab and switch Text Direction to "Right to Left (RTL)" to preview how your post reads in RTL languages like Arabic or Hebrew.
