FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the TikTok post preview tool.

Is the TikTok post preview tool free? +
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. Use it as many times as you want.
Does it upload my images or text anywhere? +
Never. All previewing happens entirely inside your browser. Your images, captions, and data are never uploaded to any server.
Can I preview TikTok photo slideshows? +
Yes. Select Photo Slideshow in the Post Type field, then upload up to 10 images. The slide counter dots will appear in the preview.
Can I preview TikTok LIVE posts? +
Yes. Switch the Post Type to LIVE to preview the red LIVE badge, live viewer count, and the overall layout as it appears in the feed.
Can I download the TikTok preview as an image? +
Yes. Click the Screenshot button at the top to download a high-quality PNG of your post preview using html2canvas.
How do I add sticker overlays? +
Go to the Style tab and click any sticker chip (Trending, Poll, Q&A, Countdown, Link) to toggle them on or off in the preview.
Does it highlight hashtags and mentions? +
Yes. Any #hashtag or @mention in your caption is automatically highlighted in TikTok teal, matching how the real app renders captions.
Can I preview the comment drawer? +
Yes. Add custom comments in the Engage tab, then set Show Comment Drawer to "Yes" to see the comment sheet slide up in the preview.
